Professional Orientation

A One-Person Technical Think Tank

The consulting model is intentionally lean. Research, engineering, analysis, design, documentation, and strategic direction remain closely integrated under one principal consultant.

Cross-Domain Engineering

The ability to move between software, embedded systems, hardware, web infrastructure, AI, Bitcoin, security, and design reduces coordination failure and fragmented responsibility.

Independent Judgment

Recommendations are made according to technical merit, operational requirements, security, maintainability, cost, risk, and long-term client interests.

Research-Led Practice

Engagements may begin with investigation, feasibility, architecture, threat modeling, or technical research before implementation is recommended.

Direct Accountability

Clients work directly with the principal consultant, reducing handoffs, internal ambiguity, hidden staffing, and communication loss.

Private Engagements

Sensitive projects may use NDA-first intake, encrypted communications, minimal-data workflows, compartmentalized documentation, and controlled disclosure.

Bitcoin-First Operations

Bitcoin is the preferred payment and treasury standard, with address, amount, confirmation, timing, and settlement terms documented before work begins.

Engagement Terms

Private, Scoped, and Prepaid

Consulting engagements are accepted selectively and begin only after scope, responsibilities, confidentiality, payment, and operational requirements have been documented.

NDA-First

Confidentiality terms may be established before sensitive project details, credentials, evidence, or internal documentation are exchanged.

Written Scope

Objectives, deliverables, exclusions, assumptions, dependencies, access, timeline, and acceptance conditions are defined in writing.

Bitcoin Payment

Bitcoin is the preferred settlement method. Payment or retainer requirements are confirmed before scheduled work begins.

Lawful Purpose

Requests are reviewed for lawful purpose, technical fit, risk, available capacity, and alignment with professional boundaries.

No automatic engagement: Sending an email, document, NDA, payment request, project archive, or technical material does not create a consultant-client relationship. An engagement exists only after explicit written acceptance.
Private Consultation

Discuss a Technical Engagement

Provide a concise description of the problem, desired outcome, technical environment, known constraints, urgency, and any confidentiality requirements. Do not send credentials, private keys, sensitive evidence, or restricted data in the initial message.
