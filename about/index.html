Who We Are

At [0xdeadbeef] Consulting, we design the technological future with precision, autonomy, and sovereignty in mind.

Our Mission

To empower clients with advanced, open-source-driven software and hardware solutions rooted in cyber defense, Bitcoin innovation, and AI autonomy.

Our Philosophy

We are a sovereign-first, privacy-respecting, Bitcoin-native consulting firm. We embrace open-source development, cryptographic accountability, and decentralized system design.

All services are provided with zero trust assumptions, national security-grade rigor, and an uncompromising commitment to digital autonomy.



Core Domains

  • • Bitcoin Software, Hardware, and Mining Infrastructure
  • • Offensive and Defensive Cybersecurity (CySec & CyWar)
  • • Open-Source Intelligence (OSINT) Tools and Analysis
  • • Large-Scale AI/ML Systems (LLMs, GANs, GPT PDAs)
  • • Full Stack Web, Firmware, and Hardware Engineering
  • • Digital Art, Music, and Writing Consulting


Operational Model

[0xdeadbeef] Consulting is structured for minimal staffing with maximal output. Each division leverages automation, AI-assistance, and decentralized workflows to accomplish secure, scalable deployments for global clientele.

We function as both an elite R&D lab and an operational think tank supporting national defense, private enterprise, and emerging decentralized ecosystems.



Global Reach

With roots in the U.S. and India, our services are offered globally through encrypted channels, VPN-tunneled infrastructure, and mirrored web deployments (.io/.onion).

All contracts are Bitcoin-denominated, prepaid, and zero-dependency — built to endure censorship and collapse.



Our Ethos

Inspired by Chomsky, Antonopolous, Schrodinger, and Vinay Gupta — we believe that freedom in the digital world is achieved not by policy, but by protocol.

We do not obey; we code.
